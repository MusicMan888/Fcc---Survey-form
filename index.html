Songwriter Meet Up Survey Form

We would like to create a local songwriting community to share experiences, ideas, enhance creativity - perhaps by challenging yourself to write 20 new songs in 12 hours. Known as the Immersion Music Method, the idea is: by imposing a seemingly impossible goal you can break free of the chains of perfectionism and get past the inner-critic that often results in writers' block.
